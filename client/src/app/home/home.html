About Tuklas

Research & Development (R&D) is a well known driver of economic progress. This is true in most advanced countries like the US, Japan, and Germany, where the intensity of R&D expenditure has been relatively high and stable for many years. Unfortunately in the Philippines, R&D has been left behind in both scope and pace.

That's why we've come up with Tuklas, an online community for researchers and funders to collaborate in order to leapfrog R&D in the Philippines.

Are you a university researcher or an indie inventor? Are you an individual or entity wanting to fund research projects? You've come to the right place!

Tuklas brings researchers and funders together to collaborate. By leveraging the collective power of the people (through crowd-funding), meaningful and high-impact research and inventions come to life, may it be in the areas of science, engineering, medicine, and governance.

Launching mid 2016.
